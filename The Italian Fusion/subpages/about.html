Meet the Wizards that brew our delicacies:


The Italian Fusion was first founded in 1996 by Chef S. Chakraborty. Its first outlet was opened at London with the hope of giving pure Italian taste in an English country. Soon the restaurant started booming with guests who were particularly excited about the unlimited food buffet concept. The Italian Fusion Restaurants Ltd. has now over 260 restaurants spread across the globe in 15 different countries.
